4.1 Installation Best Practices

  • Use shrink tubing for all electrical connections
  • Install inline fuse (3A recommended for protection)
  • Ensure clear sky view for optimal GPS antenna reception
  • Test engine immobilization before vehicle delivery
  • Provide SIM access panel for easy replacement
  • Waterproof enclosure essential for monsoon protection
